Are you here for productivity?

Increase productivity by as much as 30% by structuring your work around activity breaks. Work uninterrupted on the task at hand for 20 minutes, then take a 60-second break. Brief activity breaks jumpstart cerebral blood flow to boost your energy, focus, creativity, and brain function.

Or for your health?

Physical inactivity--seated or standing--is the #1 cause of chronic disease and early death. One hour of inactivity has the same effect on you as smoking two cigarettes. Exercise and healthy eating cannot reverse these effects. But taking short activity breaks every 20 minutes lowers your risk of all chronic disease and makes you feel better.
